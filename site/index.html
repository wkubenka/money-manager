Astute Money is a desktop app for managing your conscious spending plan, tracking net worth, and categorizing expenses — all based on Ramit Sethi’s I Will Teach You To Be Rich methodology.

Everything you need for a Rich Life

Built around the four pillars of conscious spending, so your money goes where you actually want it.

Conscious Spending Plans

Allocate your income across Fixed Costs, Investments, Savings, and Guilt-Free spending. See at a glance if you’re within ideal ranges.

Net Worth Tracking

Track assets, investments, savings, and debt in one place. Watch your net worth grow over time with a clear, honest picture of your finances.

Expense Tracking

Log expenses by account, categorize by spending bucket, and import transactions from CSV. Smart auto-categorization learns from your habits.

CSV Import

Import bank and credit card statements with automatic column detection, duplicate prevention, and bulk merchant categorization.

Private by Design

Your data lives on your computer. No accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking. Just a native desktop app that respects your privacy.

Open Source

Fully open source. Inspect the code, contribute features, or fork it and make it your own. No black boxes with your finances.
