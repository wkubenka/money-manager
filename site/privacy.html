Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

Your Data Stays on Your Computer

Astute Money is a desktop application. All of your financial data — spending plans, account balances, expenses, and settings — is stored locally on your computer in a SQLite database. Nothing is sent to any server.

What We Collect

Nothing. Astute Money does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data externally. There are no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, and no tracking of any kind.

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not sell your personal data. Ever.
  • We do not share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not transmit any data from your computer.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a local SQLite database within the application’s data directory on your machine. You have full control over this file — you can back it up, move it, or delete it at any time.

This Website

This marketing site is a static HTML page hosted on AWS. It does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. The only external resource loaded is the Tailwind CSS CDN and Google Fonts.

Open Source

Astute Money is open source. You can inspect the entire codebase on GitHub to verify these claims for yourself.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
